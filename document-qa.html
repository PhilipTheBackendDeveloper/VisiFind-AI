How to Use Document Q&A

1. Upload Your Document

Start by uploading a document (PDF, DOCX, or TXT) using the upload button or by dragging and dropping your file into the upload area.

2. Preview Your Document

Once uploaded, you can preview your document and navigate through its pages to verify the content.

3. Ask a Question

Type your question about the document content in the question box and click "Ask Question" to get an AI-powered answer.

4. Review Answers

The AI will analyze your document and provide answers based on the content. Answers will be displayed with relevant excerpts from the document.

Note: The AI works best with clear, specific questions about the content of your document. For complex documents, consider asking multiple focused questions rather than a single broad one.
