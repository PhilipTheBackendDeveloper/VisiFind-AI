How to Use Video Recognition

1. Start Detection

Click the "Start Detection" button to enable your camera and begin real-time object detection.

2. Adjust Settings

Use the confidence threshold slider to filter out low-confidence detections. Higher values mean fewer but more accurate detections.

3. Take Screenshots

Click the "Take Screenshot" button to capture the current frame with detection results.

4. View Results

Detection results appear in real-time in the right panel, showing detected objects and their confidence scores.

Note: This feature uses TensorFlow.js and runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server, ensuring your privacy. The COCO-SSD model can detect 80 common object categories.
